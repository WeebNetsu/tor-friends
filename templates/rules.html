Site Rules

Short Version

  • No CP
  • No Gore
  • No Other Illegal Stuff
  • Only private torrents
  • Don't share torrents
  • Seed

Long Version

It goes without saying, illegal stuff is obviously off-limits, except for copyrighted content such as movies. Child pornography, gore, viruses, or anything of the sort is NOT allowed. In terms of torrents, the only torrents you're allowed to add are torrents you created yourself, torrents you found on other websites such as The Pirate Bay are strictly not allowed. Torrents on this website are not allowed to be shared anywhere else. If you torrent something, seed it if you are a leecher and we find out about it, you'll be permanently banned, immediately. Things such as movies/videos/porn, games, anime/hentai are all allowed.

Breaking any of the above rules can and probably will result in a permanent ban from the website. These rules also apply to the mods, if they break it then their mod status will be removed or they will be removed from the website entirely.

Questions?

If you're on this website, it means you're on the group as well, if you have any questions, ask there and a mod will help you.

FAQs

How do I create a torrent?

It's fairly simple, here is a video that explains it: Right Here

Why keep the website so private?

It's safe, keeping torrents (especially copyrighted ones) between only a few users keeps the copyright people away, no US$5000 bills or threats.

Why am I getting an "Internal Server Error"?

The server is very slow and we lack the funds to upgrade it. If you get the error, try reloading the page.

What is seeding?

After you fully downloaded a torrent, you stay connected to that torrent so someone else can download the torrent from you later.

I don't have a constant internet connection

Fine, just put that in the description of all your torrents and seed the torrent whenever someone asks you to on the group. Note that if you fail to ever seed the file, we'll remove it and if you're being suspected of being a leecher we'll remove your account.

How do I stay a good torrent owner?

Simple, seed it as much as possible and if you're the only seed and you want to remove it or lost the file, delete it from the site.

Can I invite my friend?

If you're a mod.

How do I create a new account?

You can't, only mods can. If you forgot your password and/or username, contact a mod.

I added a new torrent, but don't see it anywhere.

Server may be a little slow, if it doesn't appear after a day, add it again.

A mod/user is harrasing me.

Contact a mod and report them immediately (remember proof).

Someone asked me for my account details.

Unless you are trying to get back into your account after forgetting your password, no one will ever ask you for your details. Only give details to mods in the group, if someone claims to be a mod but isn't in the group, report and ignore them.

I want a new feature.

Suggest the feature on the group and it will be considered.

How can I help?

Seed and report. If you know HTML, SCSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Python or Flask then you can ask a dev on the group to let you help with building the website. Or donate here: PayPal.

A torrent has no seeds!

Ask the group to seed it.

Why is the site so basic?

Loads fast, doesn't need to be the NASA of websites.
